Case 2: The Caesar Cipher

What is the Caesar Cipher?

The Caesar Cipher is one of the oldest encryption techniques, used by Julius Caesar to protect military messages. It works by shifting each letter in the plaintext by a fixed number down the alphabet. For example, with a shift of 3: A → D, B → E, ..., Z → C.
